Cranberry sauce

Description

Cranberry sauce or cranberry jam[1] is a sauce or relish made out of cranberries, commonly served as a condiment or a side dish with Thanksgiving dinner in North America and Christmas dinner in the United Kingdom and Canada.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces cranberries
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup orange juice
  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Dissolve sugar in the orange juice in a medium saucepan over medium heat
  3. Stir in the cranberries and cook until they start to pop, about 10 minutes
  4. Remove from heat and place sauce in a bowl. It will thicken as it cools
  5. Enjoy!
